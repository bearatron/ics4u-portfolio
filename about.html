About me

My name is Ethan Diep. I'm a tenth grade student from William Lyon Mackenzie. In my spare time, I enjoy playing the piano and coding. I also play badminton on the weekends. My coding journey started when I began learning HTML and CSS in middle school from Khan Academy. This is where I got most of my web-dev knowledge and was my first real exposure to programming. I then learned Processing in Grade 9 for the ICS2OP course. I also picked up Python over that summer because I wanted to learn a more widely used programming language. I am currently learning Java as part of the Grade 12 ICS4UP course. My dream is to one day become a full-time programmer.
